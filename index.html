FAQ

Quick answers before you book.

What is the seating capacity?

The grand A/C hall seats around 700 guests, and the dining hall can serve around 200 guests at a time.

Are rooms available for families?

Yes. Santhosh Mahal has 10 A/C rooms, including deluxe and VIP room options for wedding guests and family members.

Do you support catering and decoration?

The venue has a modern kitchen and pantry. Decoration can be arranged based on your event theme and stage requirements.
